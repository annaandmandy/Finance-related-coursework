### Effective Annual Interest Rates
- EAR(有效年利率) 用複利來看
    - EAR = (1 + MR)^12^ - 1
    - MR = monthly rate
- APR(年百利率) 單利
    - APR = MR * 12 
- Example
    - Given a monthly rate of 1%, what is the Effective Annual Rate(EAR)? What is the Annual Percentage Rate (APR)?p146
        - EAR = (1+.01)^12^ -1 =.1268 or 12.68%
        - APR = 0.01 * 12 = 0.12 or 12%
